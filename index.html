I am Hsuan-Cheng Wu 吳軒丞. I go with both Hsuan-Cheng (pronounced as Shuan-cheng) or Alex. I am a Ph.D. student at The Pennsylvania State University, Department of Mathematics. My research interests include but not limited to numerical analysis, scientific and quantum computation. My advisor is Dr. Xiantao Li.

My research currently focuses on Hamiltonian simulation for large-scale dynamics, quantum algorithms for linear and nonlinear problems, and the simulation of physical models on near-term quantum hardware.
